Support

Need help? We’re here. Email support@honeycomblab.art and we’ll get back to you.

What To Include

  • Your name and best reply-to email.
  • Order details (if applicable): purchase date, product name, and any license info.
  • A clear description of the issue, steps to reproduce, and screenshots if helpful.

Response Times

We aim to respond within 1–2 business days. Complex issues may take longer; we’ll keep you updated.

Licensing & Purchases

Questions about usage, upgrades, or stems? Contact us at support@honeycomblab.art. See Terms for licensing details.

Refunds

Digital goods are generally non-refundable. If you have a technical issue with a file, reach out and we’ll help resolve it.

Technical Issues

Having trouble with downloads or playback? Tell us your device, browser/DAW, and version. We’ll investigate.

Data & Privacy

For privacy requests (access, correction, deletion), see our Privacy policy or email support@honeycomblab.art.
